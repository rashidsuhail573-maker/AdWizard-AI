FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started with AdWizard AI.

What types of businesses do you work with?
We work with businesses across all industries — e-commerce, real estate, healthcare, education, hospitality, and more. Whether you're a local startup in Srinagar or a growing enterprise, we build automation systems tailored to your specific needs and goals.
How long does it take to see results?
Most clients see measurable improvements within 30 days. Full automation systems are typically deployed within 2–6 weeks. For ad campaigns, optimised performance is usually achieved within 30–45 days of launch.
What is the minimum budget for Google or Meta Ads?
We recommend a minimum ad spend of ₹15,000–₹20,000/month for Google Ads and ₹10,000–₹15,000/month for Meta Ads to see meaningful results. Our management fees are discussed during the free discovery call.
Do I need technical knowledge to use the systems you build?
Absolutely not. We build everything to be user-friendly and provide complete training and documentation. Our systems are designed to run on autopilot — you see results in your dashboard and inbox without any technical complexity.
Do you provide ongoing support after deployment?
Yes. All packages include ongoing monitoring, maintenance, and optimisation. We also provide dedicated WhatsApp support for quick queries. Our AI systems are continuously improved based on real performance data.
Can you integrate with our existing tools?
Yes — we integrate with 200+ tools including WhatsApp Business, Zoho CRM, HubSpot, Salesforce, Google Sheets, Notion, Slack, and many more. We enhance your existing stack, not replace it.
